Why Pencilbox?
What makes us, us

Sworn enemies of the shifting paradigm, PencilBox exists to return thinking to inside the box, where it belongs. We are dedicated to openness, candor and clarity in corporate communications, and we revere your audience, keeping their values, attitudes and interests uppermost in our minds.

We do what it takes reach that audience; we don't confine ourselves to one medium. Sock puppets, blimps, brochures, ads, websites, booths and bobble head dolls are only a few of our areas of expertise. Focusing on the message and the audience lets us craft materials that work, be they for big companies like The Coca–Cola Company and General Motors or non-profits like the Hispanic Heritage Foundation.

Our inclinations and experience have caused us to focus on environmental communications. Responsible environmental actions are within the capabilities of business leaders. By helping companies tell an accurate and compelling story about these actions we hope to not only help these companies get the word out, but to start conversations about what we all should be doing.

These are a few of our favorite... clients.
What, you thought we would list someone else's clients?

PencilBox is a collection of like‐minded individuals focused on delivering outstanding work for our clients. From senior management road shows to intranets and employee communications, national ad campaigns to Web sites, we've pretty much done it all. We've gathered at PencilBox because of some commonly held beliefs. While we're agnostic about the medium through which our work is delivered, we're fanatically devoted to the principles of openness, candor and clarity in corporate communication. What that translates into is the creation of corporate communication materials that work. We develop communication strategies and executions across a wide variety of media for big companies like The Coca‐Cola Company and General Motors, and non-profits like the Hispanic Heritage Foundation.

Seeing corporations adopt responsible environmental actions is one of our main goals at PencilBox. We believe that responsible environmental actions are well within the capabilities of business leaders. We are not Pollyannas; however, we believe that substantive environmental change will be driven by for‐profit enterprises. We've worked with leaders who not only believe this, but are making it a part of their business processes.
